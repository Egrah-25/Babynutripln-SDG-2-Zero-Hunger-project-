About Sustainable Development Goal 2: Zero Hunger

SDG 2 aims to end hunger, achieve food security and improved nutrition, and promote sustainable agriculture by 2030. Our project supports this goal by making nutritious baby food accessible and affordable while helping fight malnutrition in vulnerable communities.
